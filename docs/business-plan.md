# Freelance Web Business Plan (Northwest Arkansas)

## Overview

- **Business concept:** Faith-rooted freelance website studio focused on practical, conversion-friendly websites for small businesses.
- **Service area:** Northwest Arkansas (`Fayetteville`, `Bentonville`, `Rogers`, `Springdale`).
- **Core promise:** Clear messaging, trustworthy design, and ongoing support that helps small businesses get more calls, form leads, and bookings.

## Target Market

- Local small businesses that need credibility, better lead flow, and less DIY stress.
- Priority verticals to start with:
  - Home services
  - Professional services
  - Restaurants and retail
  - Health and wellness providers

## Offer Structure

### 1) Launch Package

- **Price range:** `$2,400-$3,800` one-time
- **Best for:** New or outdated sites needing a clean, professional relaunch
- **Includes:**
  - Discovery + sitemap
  - Up to 5 pages
  - Mobile optimization
  - Basic on-page SEO
  - Contact form + analytics setup
  - 1 revision round
- **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

### 2) Growth Package

- **Price range:** `$4,500-$7,500` one-time
- **Best for:** Businesses needing stronger lead generation and local visibility
- **Includes:** Everything in Launch, plus:
  - Conversion-focused page structure
  - One integration (booking, email capture, or CRM form routing)
  - 2-3 additional pages
  - Local SEO enhancements
  - 2 revision rounds
- **Timeline:** 4-8 weeks

### 3) Partner Package

- **Price range:** `$8,000-$12,000+` one-time
- **Best for:** Multi-service or scaling businesses that need deeper support
- **Includes:** Everything in Growth, plus:
  - Expanded site architecture
  - Advanced integration scope
  - Reporting and optimization support
  - Priority support model

## Recurring Revenue (Care Plans)

- **Essentials:** `$129-$229/mo`
  - Updates, backups, security, monitoring, small monthly edits
- **Pro:** `$279-$449/mo`
  - Essentials plus more monthly edit time and quarterly recommendations
- **Growth:** `$499-$899+/mo`
  - Pro plus landing page optimization and deeper analytics support

## Sales Model

- Sell outcomes (calls, forms, bookings), not just pages/features.
- Keep scope clear with revision limits and explicit boundaries.
- Offer smaller scoped entry points instead of discounting.

## Payment Terms

- Standard structure: `50%` deposit to schedule, `50%` before launch.
- Optional larger-project structure: `40/40/20`.

## Discovery Call Framework

1. Clarify business goals and ideal customers.
2. Identify current online pain points.
3. Define success outcomes and timeline urgency.
4. Confirm budget range and decision process.
5. Recommend package and next step proposal.

## 30-Day Execution Plan

### Week 1
- Select niche focus and finalize 3 package offers.
- Prepare proposal template and client onboarding checklist.

### Week 2
- Build personal agency site with clear positioning and CTA.
- Publish service and pricing anchor language.

### Week 3
- Outreach to at least 30 local businesses with personalized notes.
- Offer quick website audits to open conversations.

### Week 4
- Close first 1-2 clients.
- Collect testimonial and refine offer based on objections.

## Positioning Statement (Draft)

I help small businesses in Northwest Arkansas launch websites that are clear, trustworthy, and built to generate real leads. My process combines practical design, conversion-focused structure, and ongoing support so business owners can stay focused on serving customers.

## Key Metrics to Track

- Discovery calls booked per month
- Proposal acceptance rate
- Average project value
- Monthly recurring revenue from care plans
- Lead-to-client conversion rate
